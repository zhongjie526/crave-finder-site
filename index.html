CraveFinder

Describe what you're craving. Find the best food nearby.

How it works

1

Describe your craving

Type anything — "something spicy", "sushi", "Bugis 附近的烧烤". Use any language. Be as vague or specific as you want.

2

AI finds what you mean

An on-device AI understands your craving and searches for matching restaurants. Mention a neighborhood and it searches there.

3

Pick a spot and go

Browse results sorted by distance or rating. Preview photos, reviews, and hours. Tap to navigate in Google Maps.

Features

📍

Search any area

Mention a neighborhood — "ramen in Bukit Panjang", "pizza near Orchard" — and CraveFinder searches there, not just where you are.

🌏

Any language

Type in English, Chinese, Malay, or any language. The AI understands what you're craving regardless of how you say it.

👁️

Preview before you go

Photos, reviews, opening hours, contact info, and editorial summaries — all in one tap. Then navigate straight there.

🔀

Sort & filter

Switch between distance and rating. Filter by cuisine type. Adjust search radius from 1km to 20km.

⚡

Quick chips

Not sure what you want? Tap a suggestion chip — Sushi, Spicy, Dessert, Burgers — and discover something new.

📶

Works offline

Recent searches are cached on-device. Lose signal? Your results still work. Cache refreshes when you move to a new area.

🔒

Private by design

Everything runs on your device. Your cravings, your location, your conversations — none of it leaves your phone. No account. No login. No tracking. No ads. The AI model runs locally, not in the cloud. We don't collect any data because there's nothing to collect.
