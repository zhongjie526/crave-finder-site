Frequently Asked Questions

Why does CraveFinder need to download a model?

CraveFinder uses an AI model that runs entirely on your device to understand your cravings. The model (~1.8GB) is downloaded once during first launch. After that, the AI works offline — your conversations never leave your phone.

The download was interrupted. What do I do?

Just reopen the app. The download will resume from where it left off. Make sure you have a stable internet connection and enough storage space (~2GB free).

Why are no restaurants showing up?

A few things to check:

  • Location access: Make sure CraveFinder has permission to access your location (Settings → Privacy → Location Services → CraveFinder).
  • Internet connection: Restaurant search requires an internet connection. Cached results work offline, but new searches need Wi-Fi or mobile data.
  • Search radius: Try increasing the search radius in Settings (up to 20km).
  • Be more specific: Try describing a specific food or cuisine instead of a very broad query.

Can I search for food in a different area?

Yes! Just mention the location in your message. For example: "ramen in Bukit Panjang", "pizza near Orchard Road", or "Bugis 附近的烧烤". CraveFinder will search near that area instead of your current location.

What languages does CraveFinder support?

You can type your cravings in any language — English, Chinese, Malay, Tamil, Japanese, Korean, and more. The AI will do its best to understand what you're looking for.

Does CraveFinder work on iPad?

Yes, CraveFinder is a universal app that works on both iPhone and iPad.

Is my data private?

Completely. The AI runs on your device. Your cravings and conversations are never sent to any server. No account, no tracking, no ads. See our Privacy Policy for details.
