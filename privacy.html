Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

The short version

CraveFinder does not collect, store, or sell any personal data. Your cravings are processed via Google's Gemini AI to understand what you want, then nearby restaurants are found using Google Places. We do not operate any servers or databases.

Data we collect

None. We do not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or diagnostics. We do not operate any backend servers.

How AI works in CraveFinder

CraveFinder uses Google's Gemini API to understand your food cravings and generate search keywords. When you type a message:

  • Your message is sent to Google's Gemini API to interpret your craving
  • Gemini returns a short response and food search keywords
  • These keywords are then used to search Google Places for nearby restaurants

Your messages are processed by Google's Gemini API under Google's API Terms of Service. Google states that data sent through the paid Gemini API is not used to train their models. We do not store, log, or have access to your conversations — they go directly from your device to Google's servers.

Location

CraveFinder requests your location solely to find nearby restaurants. Your location is:

  • Sent to Google Places API as coordinates to find restaurants nearby
  • Never stored on any server we operate
  • Never shared with any third party beyond Google's APIs

You can deny location access and still use the app by mentioning a location in your message (e.g., "ramen in Bukit Panjang").

Google Services

CraveFinder uses the following Google services:

  • Gemini API — to understand your food cravings and generate search keywords
  • Google Places API — to find nearby restaurants, retrieve reviews, photos, and hours
  • Google Maps SDK — to display restaurant locations on a map

All interactions with Google's services are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. No account information or personal identifiers are included in these requests.

No accounts

CraveFinder does not require or support user accounts, logins, sign-ups, or any form of registration.

No tracking or advertising

CraveFinder contains no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no tracking pixels, and no third-party data collection of any kind.

Cached data

Search results are cached locally on your device to improve performance. This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted. You can clear the cache at any time from Settings.

Children's privacy

CraveFinder does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since the app collects no data from anyone, this applies equally to all users.

Changes to this policy

If we change this privacy policy, we will update this page with the new date.
