Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

The short version

CraveFinder does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything runs on your device.

Data we collect

None. We do not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or diagnostics.

On-device AI

CraveFinder uses an AI model that runs entirely on your iPhone or iPad. Your conversations and cravings are processed locally and never sent to any server. The model file is downloaded once during setup and runs offline thereafter.

Location

CraveFinder requests your location solely to find nearby restaurants. Your location is:

  • Used only on your device to search for nearby places
  • Sent to Google Places API as coordinates to find restaurants (Google's standard API — no personal identifiers attached)
  • Never stored on any server we operate
  • Never shared with any third party beyond the Google Places API request

You can deny location access and still use the app by mentioning a location in your message (e.g., "ramen in Bukit Panjang").

Google Places API

CraveFinder uses Google Places API to find restaurants. When you search for food, your approximate location and search keywords are sent to Google's servers to retrieve restaurant results. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. No account information or personal identifiers are included in these requests.

No accounts

CraveFinder does not require or support user accounts, logins, sign-ups, or any form of registration.

No tracking or advertising

CraveFinder contains no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no tracking pixels, and no third-party data collection of any kind.

Cached data

Search results are cached locally on your device to improve performance and enable offline access. This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted. You can clear the cache at any time from Settings.

Children's privacy

CraveFinder does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since the app collects no data from anyone, this applies equally to all users.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update this page. Given that CraveFinder collects no data, we don't anticipate significant changes.
